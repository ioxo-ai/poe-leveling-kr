# General tips

A Regex to Highlight your prefered Links and Weapons, aswell as Movement Speed boots									
Example
- Caster Regex		`b-b-|-b-b|b-.-b|nne|rint|ll g`
- Ranger Regex		`g-g-|-g-g|g-.-g|nne|rint|s:.+bow`
- Melee Regex		`r-r-|-r-r|r-.-r|nne|rint|s:.+(ax|sw)`
